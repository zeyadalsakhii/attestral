# Trademark policy

The "Attestral" and "Attestral Labs" names, the seal and plate marks, and the
wordmarks in `website/assets/` identify Attestral Labs and its products. The
Apache 2.0 license grants rights to the code; per its Section 6 it grants no
rights to these names and marks.

## You may, without asking

- State truthfully that your product or service uses, integrates, or is built
  with Attestral ("built with Attestral", "powered by Attestral scan results").
- Redistribute unmodified releases under the Attestral name, including as a
  package in a distro or registry.
- Use the name in articles, talks, benchmarks, and comparisons.

## You may not, without written permission

- Use "Attestral" (or a confusingly similar name) as the name of a fork, a
  derivative product, a hosted service, a company, or a domain.
- Use the seal, plate, or wordmark assets to brand a product that is not
  Attestral, or in a way that implies endorsement by Attestral Labs.
- Modify the marks.

A fork must be renamed and de-branded; keeping "based on Attestral" in its
description is welcome. Questions and permission requests: open a discussion on
the repository or contact the maintainer.
